# Day 1 – Assignment

## Introduction to Generative AI

### Objective

This assignment is designed to ensure that students understand the **fundamentals of Generative AI**, its evolution, real-world use-cases, and ecosystem components.

---

## Task 1: Conceptual Understanding

Answer the following questions in your own words.

1. What is Generative AI? How is it different from traditional AI systems?
2. Explain the evolution of AI from rule-based systems to Large Language Models.
3. What role do transformers play in modern Generative AI models?

---

## Task 2: Use-Case Analysis

Choose **any two** real-world applications of Generative AI and answer:

1. What problem does the application solve?
2. How does Generative AI improve the solution compared to traditional software?
3. What risks or limitations could exist in this use-case?

Examples of domains:

* Education
* Healthcare
* Customer support
* Software development
* Business automation

---

## Task 3: Ecosystem Mapping

Create a simple diagram or written explanation that includes:

* Foundation model
* API or model access layer
* Tooling/framework
* Vector database (optional)
* End-user application

Explain how data flows between these components.

---

## Task 4: Reflection (Short Answer)

Write a short reflection (150–200 words):

* How do you think Generative AI will impact your field of study or career?
* What excites you most about learning GenAI?

---
